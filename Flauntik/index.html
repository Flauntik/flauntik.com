Lighting Up Cities with Smart Screens and Smarter Data

Flauntik’s digital tops engage urban audiences with the power of digital OOH advertising.From car top advertising to car wraps, we provide brands with the tools to connect with people on their daily journeys.

WELCOME TO FLAUNTIK!

Flauntik’s mission is to bring dynamic storytelling to the streets of our cities.

Flauntik is a data-first ad network offering advanced tracking, measurement, and optimization in digital out-of-home advertising. Our dynamic and creative mobility advertising solutions deliver targeted messages at the right time.

From car tops to car wraps, Taxi TV to LED Trucks, we empower brands to engage with audiences on their daily journeys, leveraging the freedom and flexibility that mobility advertising provides.

Our GPS-enabled digital tops on top of taxis and rideshare vehicles allow brands to engage consumers efficiently in major North American cities like New York, Las Vegas, Los Angeles, Chicago, San Francisco, while our taxi/rideshare wraps, Digital LED trucks, and experiential solutions provide nationwide coverage.

OUR SOLUTIONS

On-Car
Products

  • Flauntik’s industry-leading digital top network offers the combined power of sharp quality, full-motion screens, with a cutting-edge data system that allows pre-campaign strategies to deliver measurably effective results.

  • An iconic and timeless favorite among leading brands, Flauntik’s static taxi tops provide 100% SOV (Share of Voice) and deliver reach and frequency to the most trafficked points of interest across top markets.

  • Quality creative and intentional messaging drive meaningful brand connections. Flauntik offers full (360°),  partial (270°) and door (180°) wraps for vehicles with or without digital tops.

Learn more

Car advertising and automobile advertising are popular methods used by companies to advertise on cars, through car advertisements or car ads. With car wrap advertising, it is easy to advertise on cars, making them an effective platform for car advertisement. Whether it's ads on cars or a broader campaign, car advertise strategies offer a great way to reach customers on the go.

In-Car
Products

  • Since 2007, TaxiTV has accompanied riders to provide an effortless transit experience, tapping into local NBC news and entertainment programming.

  • Flauntik’s HD touchscreen golf screens include built-in speakers and bluetooth connectivity offering golf enthusiasts GPS yardage, real-time sports tickers, music streaming, and scorecards… all while displaying advertising.

Learn more

Around-Car
Products

  • For a high-impact presence in targeted areas, Flauntik’s multi-vehicle takeover on a custom route with 100% share of voice can create lasting impressions. 

  • Enhance consumer engagement with complimentary transportation in Flauntik’s wrapped vehicles or digital top taxis, turning every interaction into a memorable brand experience.

  • Flauntik’s brand ambassadors provide hands-on experiences who distribute, educate and deliver a concierge-level experience, allowing consumers to engage with your brand in their own environment.

  • Flauntik is able to maximize your reach by combining the power of out-of-home advertising with mobile retargeting, ensuring an effective brand interaction that drives results.

  • Flauntik’s cutting-edge 3D projection technology can bring your brand's story to life, using holograms to capture attention and engage audiences on the bustling streets.

  • Elevate your brand’s presence with custom vehicle build-outs that pair seamlessly with Flauntik’s tops, creating a striking mobile advertisement on rideshares and taxis.

Learn more

Experiential
Offerings

  • Stand out from the crowd with the option to run a video or static image on a high-impact LED digital screen. This is the brightest and boldest way to engage audiences!

  • Laser technology fuses with automotive ingenuity to create the illusion of motion as your brand’s message is projected alongside passed buildings on a custom route.

  • The opportunity to associate your brand with a moment of enjoyment. Branded food trucks are a multi-sensory approach to engaging consumers. They see the full exterior wrap, taste product samples, and listen to custom messaging with our audio capabilities.

  • Support an event or a product launch with a pedicab fleet swarming a location and offering rides to boost the impact of the campaign. Delight consumers with an environmentally-conscious solution to navigate a city’s most congested areas.
