Working at Firefly

Drive the light, accelerate innovation,
steer with authenticity!

Do you ever wonder how it would feel working in a place where your individuality is celebrated? This is what Firefly life means to our employees. We don't adhere to a rigid culture or set of rules; instead, we embrace individuality and diverse perspectives, fostering a sense of belonging where authenticity is valued over conformity. We encourage everyone to shine their unique light without the need to conform or conceal their true identities.

This is Firefly where authenticity fuels success, empowerment drives your journey, innovation reshapes industries, and collaboration lights our path forward. If you wonder how these are reflected in our lives, let's dive in together to explore our collective brilliance.

LIFE AT FIREFLY

We empower our employees to thrive by embracing their authentic selves, fostering a culture of individuality, and celebrating diversity. We believe that when you can be yourself at work, you can bring your best to the table. We value your unique perspectives and contributions, recognizing that every idea, no matter how unconventional, has the potential to drive innovation and progress. 

Our commitment to transparency ensures that you have access to the information and resources you need to excel, and our fearless approach to challenges creates an environment where your growth knows no bounds.

WE LIKE TO STAY CONNECTED

Our internal communication platform keeps our global team connected and engaged, fostering a sense of belonging and collaboration across regions. It simplifies managing diverse policies and offers customized support for our teams.

Collaborating effortlessly with our Firefly colleagues, we light up each other’s paths by fostering communication, cultivating a strong sense of community, engaging in gaming, celebrating achievements, sharing feedback, and keeping everyone informed with updates about our team members.

WE VALUE ENGAGEMENT

At Firefly, our cultural architects ensure everyone in our team feels valued and connected. This dedicated team focuses on internal communication and cross-departmental collaboration, prioritizing your well-being and engagement. Think of them as the behind-the-scenes maestros, orchestrating a workplace culture that's positive, inclusive, and undeniably cool. Your journey at Firefly is more than a job; it's a collective experience shaped by this dynamic community. Welcome to the heartbeat of our vibrant workplace!

INTERNAL INNOVATION COMPETITIONS

Fuel your creativity at Firefly's Innovation Arena. Internal Innovation Competitions are your stage to shine. Your ideas drive our evolution, placing you in the driver's seat of innovation. Unleash your brilliance, grow with us, and let your unique light transform concepts into reality. This is more than a competition; it's your journey to shape our future. With these types of initiatives we encourage people to work with cross functional teams and come up with ideas that would either enhance working procedures or new product ideas as you are in the driver's seat.

SOCIAL CLUBS

Envision stepping into a captivating realm where the bonds forged transcend the confines of the workplace. Picture a space where each experience is meticulously crafted to bring forth the distinct brilliance residing within every member of our team. Our vibrant Firefly Map, constantly evolving and dynamic, unfolds a diverse array of activities meticulously designed to amplify and celebrate your individuality. 

This isn't just about work; it's about creating a tapestry of moments that resonate with your unique essence. The Firefly Map is more than a guide; it's an invitation to immerse yourself in a mosaic of opportunities. You can find yourself in a football team, a music club, a book or movie discussion club from all around the world. Don’t think these are enough? Bring your new hobby in to the table and lets find your colleagues that shares your passion.

FIREFLY VALUES “YOU” AND OFFERS YOU

BENEFITS

Life at Firefly, work freely, anytime, anywhere

What distinguishes us apart from any other work place? We are a group of people who are connected globally with multiple time zones and from many different geographical locations. You can find yourself having a meeting with someone from New York, San Francisco, London and also Istanbul. 

Experience the freedom of working from anywhere at any time. At Firefly, we understand the importance of flexibility in today's dynamic world and we embrace a work environment that adapts to everyone’s lifestyles.

HEALTH & WELL-BEING

We prioritize offering comprehensive health benefits packages and free wellness app to our employees to ensure their physical and mental well-being is supported. By providing extensive coverage, we aim to promote preventive care and address any health concerns proactively. Our commitment to exceptional health benefits reflects our dedication to fostering a supportive and healthy work environment for all members of our team in all locations.

FIREFLY UNIVERSITY

At our company, we deeply value continuous learning and personal development across all areas. That's why we have created a multifaceted approach that combines various opportunities under one umbrella which is Firefly University to empower our employees to grow professionally and personally. From structured onboarding programs in our Google Classroom, Pep Talks memberships, internal training sessions to mentorship initiatives and educational reimbursement, we strive to cultivate an environment where everyone has the chance to expand their skills, knowledge, and capabilities to reach their full potential.

HOW WE HIRE

At Firefly, we believe in putting candidates firmly in the driver's seat of their journey with us. Much like embarking on a road trip where the driver determines the destination, our candidates take the lead by applying and bringing their authentic selves to our interviews. They are the ones steering their career paths, choosing the routes that align with their aspirations and skills. 


Our role is to provide a well-maintained road, offering support and guidance along the way. As they navigate through the recruitment process, we encourage them to express their true selves, just as a skilled driver maneuvers confidently on the road. Through interviews and assessments, we provide signposts and markers, allowing candidates to showcase their abilities and fit for the role. Ultimately, when the journey concludes, it's a mutual decision, acknowledging a harmonious match, as we drive into a new chapter together.

Application

Candidates kick start their journey by submitting applications, preparing to embark on a career adventure with us.

Call with a recruiter

Selected candidates engage in a chat with a recruiter, ensuring alignment between their aspirations and our company's vision.

Interview(s)

Candidates navigate through interviews, sharing their stories and skills while we explore their authentic selves.

Technical interview

Some roles require a technical detour, where candidates showcase expertise and problem-solving abilities.

Final interview

Successful candidates reach a crucial junction, meeting with senior leaders to discuss alignment with our long-term goals.

Decision

After careful deliberation, a decision emerges, marking not just a position filled but a new co-pilot welcomed on our journey ahead.

DIVERSITY AND INCLUSION

Firefly’s success is based on diversity, different ideas, and the individual skills of its employees. Diversity offers an increased variety of viewpoints and perspectives, allows for better problem solving, and contributes to Firefly’s overall effectiveness as a company. You are expected to respect diversity and foster an atmosphere of trust, fairness, openness, and candor. We place high value on a tolerant, respectful and fair working atmosphere, both internally as well as with our business partners.
